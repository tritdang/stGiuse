Our Community · Cộng Đoàn

Faith, Family &
Vietnamese Heritage

Welcome to our Vietnamese Catholic St Giuse Parish in Tampa, Florida — a vibrant community of faith rooted in the rich spiritual traditions of Vietnam and nourished by the Catholic Church.

Founded by Vietnamese immigrants seeking to preserve their faith and culture, our parish has grown into a beloved home for families, young professionals, and seniors across the Tampa Bay area.

We celebrate the Sacraments, honor the Blessed Virgin Mary, and gather as one family — honoring both our Vietnamese heritage and our life as American Catholics.
